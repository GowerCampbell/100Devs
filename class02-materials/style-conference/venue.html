The conference is held at The Chicago Theatre, a beautiful historic landmark situated in the heart of downtown Chicago.

The Chicago Theatre

Built in 1921, The Chicago Theatre was the first large, lavish movie palace in America and was the prototype for all others that followed. This beautiful venue features a grand Baroque-Revival style, with elaborate decorations both inside and out.
